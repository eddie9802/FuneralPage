Hamilton's Funeral Services

About us

For almost 100 years now Hamilton's Funeral Service have served the surrounding area. We are proud to say that we are one of the oldest established funeral undertaking firms in South Derry. The business is presently under the management of Samuel Hamilton, Grandson of the founder. Because we are a small firm we believe that in your time of need we can offer a personal and caring service from your first enquiry.

Services

We offer a wide range of services including:

  • 24-hour call-out service 365 days a year
  • Guidance and advice on making funeral arrangements
  • Arrangement of press notices
  • Printing of order of services sheets
  • Handling of donations and liaising with relevant charities
  • Home Visits / Out of Hours visits if preferred
  • Funeral home
  • Floral Tributes & Flower arrangements
  • Pre-Payment plans available
  • Funeral Catering
